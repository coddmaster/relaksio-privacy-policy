Relaksio – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 8, 2025

1. Introduction

Relaksio is a relaxation app that provides calming sounds and images to help users reduce stress and improve sleep. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

Relaksio does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or location.

2.1 Microphone Permission (RECORD_AUDIO)

  • Purpose: This permission may be used to enable optional audio features such as ambient response or playback interaction.
  • No Recording: Relaksio does not record, store, or transmit any microphone data.
  • User Control: You may revoke microphone access at any time in your device settings.

2.2 Analytics and Diagnostics

We may use third-party services such as RevenueCat to collect limited, non-personal diagnostic data (e.g., crash logs, purchase validation) to improve functionality and user experience. This data is anonymized and not linked to your identity.

3. In-App Purchases

Relaksio uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect limited device identifiers and purchase receipts to verify entitlements. We do not collect or store any credit card or payment information directly.

4. Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat – Manages in-app purchases. See their Privacy Policy.

5. No Ads or Tracking

Relaksio does not use advertising networks or third-party tracking SDKs. We do not share or sell any data for marketing purposes.

6. Children’s Privacy

Relaksio is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we may hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent (if applicable)
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Request data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at app.gmania@gmail.com

8. Legal Basis

We rely on legitimate interest or your consent (where applicable) as the legal basis for any data processing done via third-party services.

9. Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here and, where required, communicated through the app or store listing.

Contact
