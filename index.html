Relaksio – Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Relaksio is a relaxation app that provides calming sounds and images to help users reduce stress and improve sleep. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data Collection

Relaksio does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email, or location. However, we may use third-party services that automatically collect information like purchase history and app usage for functionality and improvements.

3. In-App Purchases

Relaksio uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect limited device and purchase data. We do not store any payment information directly.

4. Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat – Used to handle purchases. See their Privacy Policy.

5. Children’s Privacy

Relaksio is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Your Rights

You may contact us to request deletion of any data related to your usage or purchases, if applicable.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be communicated through the app or the App Store listing.
